German Apple Pancake

Description

German apple pancake is also known as a Dutch baby. It is a terrific baked pancake recipe for those special occasions. If you are strapped for time, you can start it the evening before and pretty much pop it in the oven. Delicious topped with powdered sugar, maple syrup, or a dollop of yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 1/2 cup unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar and salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large tart apple - peeled, cored and sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon and nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Caramelize the sliced apple. Cook the apple in a few tablespoons of melted butter, sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg until softened and lightly caramelized around the edges.
  2. Meanwhile, make the batter.Blitz all the ingredients together in a blender.
  3. Bake the pancake
